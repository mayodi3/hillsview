Our Story

A Vision Born from Love for Nature

Hills View Resort Logere was officially launched on December 26, 2021, with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a sanctuary where guests can escape the hustle of everyday life and reconnect with nature.

Nestled at the foot of the majestic Maragoli Hills in Vihiga County, our resort offers a unique blend of natural beauty, modern comfort, and authentic Kenyan hospitality. What was once a dream is now a beloved destination.

Our Purpose

Mission & Values

Our Mission: To provide exceptional hospitality experiences that blend natural beauty with modern comfort, creating lasting memories for every guest while celebrating the rich heritage of Maragoli.

Our Vision: To be recognized as the premier hospitality destination in Western Kenya, known for our commitment to guest satisfaction, sustainable practices, and authentic experiences.
