Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a reservation?

You can book by calling us, sending a WhatsApp message, or filling out the contact form above. We recommend WhatsApp for the fastest response.

Do you accept walk-in guests?

Yes, we welcome walk-in guests! However, we recommend booking in advance, especially during weekends and holidays to ensure availability.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, cash, and bank transfers. Payment can be made upon check-in or in advance for reservations.

Can I book for events or conferences?

Absolutely! We have 5 conference halls and spacious grounds for events. Contact us with your requirements and we'll provide a customized quote.

Is parking available?

Yes, we have spacious, secure parking available for all our guests at no additional cost.

How do I join a hiking trip?

Contact us to book a hiking package to Maragoli Hills. Packages include food, water, a tour guide, and photography services.
