**DIGITAL MARKETING STRATEGY FOR HILLS VIEW RESORT LOGERE (2025/2026)**

*By Starbornlab Kenya.*

**1\. BRAND POSITIONING & IDENTITY**

**Brand Identity**

**“Nature. Comfort. Experience.”**  
A refreshing nature escape at the foot of Maragoli Hills offering executive stays, vibrant experiences, conferences, events & unforgettable hospitality.

**Tone & Style**

* Non-luxury but **classy, warm & executive**  
* Simple language  
* Nature-inspired imagery  
* Heavy use of the Maragoli Hills backdrop

**Target Customer Segments**

1. **Corporate Organizations** – for conferences, meetings, retreats  
2. **Young Millennials & Gen Z** – RnB nights, Pool parties, restaurant  
3. **Families** – weekend outings, holiday offers, swimming  
4. **Tourists & Travelers** – hiking packages, cottage stays  
5. **Content Creators** – photography & lifestyle content  
6. **Schools & Churches** – camps, retreats, events

**2\. DIGITAL MARKETING OBJECTIVES (12 MONTHS)**

**Primary Goals**

* Position Hills View Resort as the *top hospitality destination* in Vihiga County

* Increase restaurant, events & recreation foot traffic

**Key KPIs**

* Monthly bookings

* Occupancy for cottages
