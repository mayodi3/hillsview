Welcome to Our Resort

Experience the Beauty of Maragoli Hills

Nestled at the foot of the majestic Maragoli Hills in Vihiga County, Hills View Resort Logere offers a unique blend of natural beauty, modern comfort, and warm Kenyan hospitality. Since our founding on December 26, 2021, we've been dedicated to creating unforgettable experiences for our guests.

Whether you're seeking a peaceful weekend getaway, planning a corporate conference, or looking for the perfect venue for your special event, our resort provides the ideal setting with stunning views, excellent amenities, and our signature hospitality.
